NIST GIAB Reference Materials


  • Well-characterized, whole genome standards
  • Guides that can tell a laboratory how well its DNA sequencing processes are working by measuring the performance of the equipment, chemistry and data analysis involved
  • RMs:
  • NIST RM 8375 — genomes for four bacterial species
  • NIST RM 8393 — male of Chinese ancestry
  • NIST RM 8392— Eastern European Ashkenazi Jewish Trio [son, father and mother] (NIST RM 8391 - son)

Central Aim


Generate a high confidence list of large indels and structural variants (SVs) [>=20bp] within the NIST human reference genome [NIST RM 8391]. Machine learning will be used to integrate data from multiple sequencing technologies to generate the benchmark set of SVs.
